ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Katherine.

I am a New Mexico native, CS @ Princeton University, and self-proclaimed foodie. My focus is primarily on the applications of CS in Neuroscience and Statistics and Machine Learning.

My curiosity about the brain developed from an early interest in medicine and involvement in bioinformatics and neurosurgery research at the Los Alamos National Laboratory, Los Alamos Medical Center, and Children's Hospital of Philadelphia. When my interest in the brain met my love for computation, I began my exploration into the fields of Data Science and ML.

I am currently a member of the Data Science Team @ Apester, researcher in the Princeton Neuroscience Insitute, organizer of HackPrinceton and Princeton MediHack, and fashion blogger @thebelpetite.
