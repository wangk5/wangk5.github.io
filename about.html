Hi, I'm Katherine.

I am interested in the applications of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Neuroscience. I was New Jersey born and New Mexico raised, and have returned to New Jersey to pursue my Bachelor's in Computer Science with a focus on Neuroscience and Statistics/Machine Learning at Princeton University.

I am a researcher in the Princeton Neuroscience Insitute, Certified Nurse Assistant, HackPrinceton and MediHack organizer, and fashion blogger (@thebelpetite).

My curiosity about the brain grew from an early interest in medicine and work experiences at the Los Alamos Medical Center and Children's Hospital of Philadelphia. I found that my love for computation and data intersected with this curiosity in the fields of Data Science and Machine Learning, which brings me to today...
